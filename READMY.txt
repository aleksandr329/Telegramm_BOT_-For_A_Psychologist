This Telegramm Bot was developed for a Psychologist in Yekaterinburg.
In the BOTE production, the logic of the psychoanalysis test is implemented.
Also this BOT with detection and the ability to detect the Database of its target audience.

In the photo folder, all the photos that are involved in the bot.
In the video folder, all the videos that participate in the bot.
All logic is written in the main.py file.
The db.py file is responsible for working with the SQLite database.
